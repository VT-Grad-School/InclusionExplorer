About Inclusive VT Explorer

This project is in cooperation with InclusiveVT, and aims to help people explore the diversity and inclusion initiatives at VT. This project would not have been possible without the help of Vice President & Dean for Graduate Education Dr. Karen DePauw, Director of Graduate Recruiting & Diversity Initiatives Dannette Gomez Beane, Graduate Assistant Stephanie House-Niamke, Associate Director of Diversity and Strategic Planning & Improvement Marcy Schnitzer, and Global Perspectives Fellow Michael Stewart.

Technical Information

This application heavily utilizes d3, having referenced the Force-Directed Graph example, to build the interactive visualization. We employ AngularJS to manage the front-end routing and to bind the data to the templates. We (will) use Node.js with the Jade Template Engine (for server-side rendering).
