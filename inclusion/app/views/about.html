About Inclusion Insights

This project hopes to help people explore the diversity and inclusion initiatives at VT. This project would not have been possible without the help of Vice President & Dean for Graduate Education Dr. Karen DePauw, Director of Graduate Recruiting & Diversity Initiatives Dannette Gomez Beane, Graduate Assistant Stephanie House-Niamke, Associate Director of Diversity and Strategic Planning & Improvement Marcy Schnitzer, and Global Perspectives Fellow Michael Stewart.
